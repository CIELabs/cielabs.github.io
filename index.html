Gotham Data Clinic

Gotham Data Clinic is a New York-based nonprofit offering data science research, education, training, and consulting services, all in the public interest.

We serve the greater New York City general public and its community organizations.

About Us: Founding Members

Teon L Brooks, Ph.D

Board Member

Teon joined Gotham Data Clinic to work full-time as the Executive Director. Prior to his current role, Teon worked at Mozilla as a staff data scientist as well as in the open-source community since 2013 developing software for brain imaging research. He has taught workshops and tutorials on hands-on experimentation, data acquisition and data analysis.

Steven J Azeka

Board Member

Steve joined Gotham Data Clinic while serving as a Program Lead for Responsible Computing at Mozilla and an Adjunct at the College of Staten Island. Prior to his current role, Steve served as a Program Coordinator for NYU BrainWaves Project. He taught science, technology, engineering, and mathematics at the elementary and high school levels in California and New York.

Mya Doelling

Board Member

Mya joined GDC while serving as a Manager of Global Partnerships at the International Olympic Committee. Prior to her current role, Mya began her career as Director of Operations for the Michael Phelps Foundation.
